Stuffed Ravioli to Stuff Your Tummy!

Made with yummy cheese and savory tomato sauce with stuffed pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Mixed tomato sauce and ground beef.
  2. Put mixture to pasta and stuff it
  3. Top with cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 mins on 120 degrees celcius
  5. Enjoy!
