Pizza

Appetizing Pizza

Made with yummy cheese and savory tomato sauce and thin crust dough.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Mixed tomato sauce and ground beef.
  2. Put mixture to dough
  3. Top with cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 mins on 120 degrees celcius
  5. Enjoy!
